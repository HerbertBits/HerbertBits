EFFECTS OF POLLUTION IN RWAMABONDO TOWN

Pollution in Rwamabondo has resulted to many threats in the town regarding on Land, Air, Water, and noise pollution. The effects cause harm to all the environment. 

EFFECTS OF LAND POLLUTION

  • Land pollution has led to infetility of the soil due to pollutants like polythenes and plastics  that are dumped on the top soil by denying water drainage.
  • Land pollution has led to death of living organisms due to toxic substances forexample detergents
  • Pollutants like polythene bags and plastics has caused stagnant of water which provide breeds for vectors spreading diseases like malaria

EFFECTS OF WATER POLLUTION

  • Water pollution has led to death of acquatic animals
  • Water pollution has led to unclean water for consumption
  • water pollution has led to the spread of water borne diseases forexample typhoid.

EFFECTS OF AIR POLLUTION

  • Air polllution has led to global warming due to the destruction of ozone layers.
  • Air pollution has led to the spread of diseases like lung cancer due to tobacco smoke and fumes from industries and vehicles.
  •   Air pollution has led to the accumulation of dust in the environment of Rwamabondo town
