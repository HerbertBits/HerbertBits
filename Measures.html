MEASURES THAT CAN BE TAKEN TO REDUCE POLLUTION IN RWAMABONDO TOWN

Pollution in Rwamabondo town according to the investigation can be controlled in the following ways;

LAND POLLUTION

  • Owners of retail shops in Rwamabondo town should have dust bins for proper disposing of plastics.
  • Land pollution in Rwamabondo town can be controlled by recycling plastic bottles.
  • Rwamabondo leaders should put strict laws against improper disposing of pollutants.
  • People of Rwamabondo town should properly dispose pollutants in order to control pollution.
  • People of Rwamabondo should Re-use polltants like polythene bags.

WATER POLLUTION

  • Treating sewage before dumping them in water sources in oder to control water pollution in Rwamabondo town.
  • Rwamabondo leaders should put strict laws against people who dump  chemicals and pollutants in water sources.
  • Rwamabondo leaders should sensitize people about proper disposal of pollutants.
  • people in Rwamabondo town should clean the water sources in order to control  water pollution
  • people in Rwamabondo should fence water bodies in order to control water pollution.

AIR POLLUTION
