POLLUTION IN RWAMABONDO TOWN,RWAMABONDO TOWN CONCIL

 

Pollution in Ramabondo has resulted from the small industries,markets and retail shops which dispose polythenes, plastic bottles and toxic gases in the environment of Rwamabondo town.This has led to many kinds of pollution in Rwamabondo town .

 

 

 

 

 

Pollution is the contamination of the environment with harmful substances that can affect living organisms. Pollution in Rwamabondo is experienced as Land, Air and Water pollution.
