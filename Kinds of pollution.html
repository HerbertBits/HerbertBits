KINDS OF POLLUTION IN RWAMABONDO

Rwamabondo town experiences different kinds of pollution and these include; Air pollution, Land pollution and Water pollution.

AIR POLLUTION

  • Air pollution is the addition of harmful substances which are toxic to air. Examples include Fumes from industries, burning of polythenes and plastics among others.

WATER POLLUTION

  • Water pollution is the addition of harmful wastes into waterbodies. Examples include polythene bags and plastic bottles.

LAND POLLUTION

  • Land pollution is the disposal of harmful wastes on and in soil which is poisonous to the ecosystem. Examples include polythen bags and other industrial wastes
